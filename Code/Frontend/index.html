This is the website for the project Race Tracking. Here you can find all the tracked data, as well as activate the lights and speakers in the car. At the moment we only support one car at the time, in future versions this will be expanded on.

Live data Tracking

Track data from your RC car live in the data page. This includes your car's current speed, temperature and how bright it is around it. You can also look up older data, sort it or find your highest or lowest peaks in a certain time frame

Data page

On board lighting & sound

Spruce up your car with the onboard NeoPixels and speaker. Just head on over to the controls page and select a LED colour or sound to play
